What Is the ROS™?

The System That Should Have Always Existed

Most diaspora returnees plan their move the same way — Google, group chats, and guesswork. Then they land, and the surprises start. The attorney had a conflict of interest. The realtor was showing fraudulent listings. The budget didn't account for currency variance.

None of that is bad luck. It's the absence of a system.

The Relocation Operating System™ is a complete, Notion-based framework that walks you through every phase of your international relocation — step by step, gate by gate. Built by someone living this transition in real time.
